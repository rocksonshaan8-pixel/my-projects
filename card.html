Lago Di Braies

Lago di Braies is a lake in the Prags Dolomites in South Tyrol, Italy. It is situated at an elevation of 1,496 metres (4,908 ft) in the Braies Valley, north of the Prags Dolomites. The lake has a maximum depth of 36 metres (118 ft) and an area of 31 hectares (77 acres). It is a popular tourist destination and is known for its clear turquoise waters and stunning mountain scenery.
